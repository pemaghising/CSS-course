About Me

I’m a Motion and Graphic Designer with over 7 years of experience bringing stories to life through design and animation. My work spans across different media — from digital platforms and social campaigns to explainer videos and brand identities.

I believe good design goes beyond aesthetics — it’s about communicating ideas clearly, creating emotion, and solving problems. Over the years, I’ve worked with a wide range of brands, teams, and industries, helping them turn concepts into visuals that connect with their audience.
